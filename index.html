Motivation

The Encounters in Econometric Theory workshop aims to bring together young econometricians across Europe in a collegial setting to discuss and share current research.

Following the success of previous editions at Nuffield College, Oxford and the Paris School of Economics, we are delighted to return to Oxford for the 2026 meeting.

Our goal is to create an intimate and stimulating environment for discussion in theoretical econometrics, while encouraging exchange across institutions and research interests.
